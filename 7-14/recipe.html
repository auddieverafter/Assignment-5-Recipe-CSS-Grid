Basque Cheesecake

If you want to learn everything about Basque Cheesecake, you are in the right place! A crustless cheesecake that originated in San Sebastián, Spain, in 1990, Basque Cheesecake is famous for its burnt, almost caramelized top layer, and custard-texture inside.
