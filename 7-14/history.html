History of Basque Cheesecake

If you want to learn the history of Basque Cheesecake, you are in the right place!

A crustless cheesecake that originated in San Sebastián, Spain, in 1990, Basque Cheesecake is famous for its burnt, almost caramelized top layer, and custard-texture inside.
